Purpose

Our purpose in establishing an online presence in our company is primarily to increase visibility, attract more potential customers and facilitate the booking of services.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

People from 18 to 45 years old, both men and women, with a varied but active educational level in search of outdoor experiences. They reside in urban and suburban areas, mainly in regions close to rivers conducive to rafting. Adventurers and nature lovers who seek strong emotions and unique experiences. They value freedom, adrenaline and connection with nature. They have an active lifestyle and enjoy challenging physical activities.
