Overview

Purpose

Our purpose in establishing an online presence in our company is primarily to increase visibility, attract more potential customers and facilitate the booking of services.

Audience

People from 18 to 45 years old, both men and women, with a varied but active educational level in search of outdoor experiences. They reside in urban and suburban areas, mainly in regions close to rivers conducive to rafting. Adventurers and nature lovers who seek strong emotions and unique experiences. They value freedom, adrenaline and connection with nature. They have an active lifestyle and enjoy challenging physical activities.

what will my audience find when they enter the website?

when they enter the site the idea is that they can find all the information for them to enroll in rafting with details of frequently asked questions and information so that they can feel confident to enroll, to see a reliable site and can resolve their questions.

How will my audience typically access my site?

Normally they will be able to access from their cell phones referred from instagram or facebook ads or already from a computer where they can see the page in the best way.

What specific information do our users look for when planning a rafting experience?

Our users will find information about the places where they can go rafting, information about the equipment they will use, travel packages with lodging included, information about rafting workshops etc.
