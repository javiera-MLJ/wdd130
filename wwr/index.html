Our Trip

Training in Noguera Pallaresa

we start with a small training where we explain all the steps to follow to have the proper security in our rafting, along with instructions to be able to have the best experience, after instructions by land will come the instructions by sea.

tempest river rafting

After our water practices, we will start with a stronger routine in our famous tempest river with your instructor, who will guide you throughout the tour, you will enjoy the best experience and the beautiful surroundings of the tempest river.

futaleufu waterfall

After spending an exciting event in the tempest river we will arrive to the futaleufu waterfall where we can enjoy the beautiful view with calm waters, you can also take the opportunity to enjoy swimming and more activities within our rafting, all this and more can be found in white water rafting.
